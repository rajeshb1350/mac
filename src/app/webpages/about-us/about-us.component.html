ABOUT US

We nuture passion to add value and provide the Best-in-Class Operational & Technological Solutions

MacTec Solutions, the name stands for Management and Technology Solutions, is a leading smart employee services company, operating from Hyderabad, India and New Jersey, USA.

Our success has been built upon having the best software developers, testers and accounting professionals in the industry to deliver a set of services tailored to what our clients want and need. We are pioneering industry-specific Smart Employee Solutions with focussed emphasis on operational excellence. Delivery of superior standard of service coupled with low cost and new age digital practices that enable rapid growth, rich value and specific business outcomes.
