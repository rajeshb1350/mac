What is
Skill Passport® ?

Skill Passport® is the gateway to a career that every student aspires for. It’s an intuitive digital platform that leverages result driven assessment and sustainable learning. Recognizing the apparent gap between the requisite skills and the existing competencies, Skill Passport bridges the disparity.

Our programs cater to various industry domains keeping in sync with the updated requirement and global standards.

In a nut shell, Skill Passport® is a digital platform that encapsulates the journey of a learner through a robust mechanism by identifying learning needs and efficiently managing the learning process for sustainable skills and competencies.
